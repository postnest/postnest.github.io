How PostNest Works

Get started in minutes with these simple steps

1

Connect Your Accounts

Sync all your social media accounts in one secure dashboard with our one-click authorization. We support Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Bluesky, Threads, and Pinterest.

2

Upload Your Media

Select photos, videos, or create content directly within the app. Import from your device gallery or cloud storage services. Our smart auto-crop ensures your content looks perfect on every platform.

3

Edit with Design Studio

Transform your content with our integrated Design Studio. Add text, filters, effects, and use our AI Caption Assistant to generate engaging captions and hashtags that drive engagement.

4

Schedule & Post

Choose specific times or let our AI suggest optimal posting times based on when your audience is most active. Schedule content weeks in advance or post immediately across all your platforms with one click.

5

Track Performance

Monitor engagement metrics, audience growth, and content performance with our intuitive analytics dashboard. Generate custom reports to optimize your strategy based on real data.
